what is friend club?

we’re a group of ppl who like to do cool stuff with our friends !
we currently run thematically-varying audiovisual events in vrchat and we host creative sessions in our discord on the first tuesday of every month.

you can find a list of our past events along with posters, descriptions, and set-recordings in the “events & updates” section of our website below.

how do i join?

upcoming events are announced here & in our discord.
you can also subscribe to our rss feed for event updates.
events are held in vrchat using our vrchat group.

if you would like assistance with attending an event or you have any questions at all, please feel free to ask in our discord or email us at staff@friendclub.lol.
