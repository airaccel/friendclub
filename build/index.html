what is friend club?

we’re a group of ppl who like to do cool stuff with our friends ! currently, we run audiovisual events in vrchat (whenever we think of something interesting), and we host creative sessions in our discord on the first tuesday of every month.

you can find a list of our past events along with posters, descriptions, and links to set uploads in the “event update” section of our website below.

how do i join?

upcoming events will be announced here & in our discord. join the vrchat group for event access. if you have any questions about vrchat or anything at all, please feel free to ask in our server or email us at staff@friendclub.lol * discord * vrchat group
